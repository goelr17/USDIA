US Direct Investment Abroad

Financial Transactions

1993

Below is a visualization of flows that makeup US direct investment abroad in 55 countries over the period 1994-2015. The US is the largest investor abroad and the largest recipient of direct investment in the world. This map depicts financial transactions, or the flows of capital from U.S. parents, the direct investor, to their foreign affiliates, the U.S. owned foreign companies. Green circles indicate outflows from the US into a country, while red circles indicate the withdrawal of US investment from the country back to the U.S.parent. A map of the US Direct investment Position is here: USDIA Position

The data is obtained from the Bureau of Economic Analysis and can be found here: BEA
