US Direct Investment Position Abroad

1993

Below is a visualization of US direct investment positions abroad in 55 countries over the period 1994-2015. This map helps show which countries host US investment and how the magnitude of US investment has changed over time. The position is a cumulative stock of investment in the country; it measures the total outstanding level of U.S. direct investment abroad at yearend. A map of the flows between U.S. parents and foreign affiliates is here: USDIA:Financial Transactions

The data is obtained from the Bureau of Economic Analysis and can be found here: BEA
